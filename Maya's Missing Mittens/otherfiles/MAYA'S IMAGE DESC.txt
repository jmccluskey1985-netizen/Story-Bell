MAYA'S MISSING MITTENS — IMAGE DESCRIPTIONS  ( 1 3 and 4 are done see attached)
============================================

This file contains the image prompts used to generate illustrations for the children’s story: 
"Maya’s Missing Mittens – A Story About Thinking Things Through".

Each image prompt is designed in a warm, hand-drawn, colorful style suitable for children ages 4–7. 
Art size: 600x300px. Main character: Maya – a 5-year-old girl with curly brown hair.

----------------------------------------------------------

----------------------------------------------------------
Children's book illustration: A cheerful 5-year-old girl named Maya with curly brown hair wearing a warm winter coat, 
holding up her hands to show bright red mittens decorated with white snowflake patterns. 
Snow is gently falling around her, and she has a big smile on her face. 
Soft, warm art style suitable for children's books.
